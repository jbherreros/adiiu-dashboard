In this bar chart you can see the top scorers of the selected year and also the relation between the goals and the shots by player. This demonstrates that normally, as more attemps a player has, more goals he scores by the end of the season.

The doughnut chart shows us the total amount of goals scored in the most important leagues by season. As we can see, there is not much difference between them all, although the Serie A, La Liga and Premier League are always the most scored leagues.

The above area chart presents the player's performance year by year. Please note that only players with four or more seasons competed are available for displaying and data for 2020 is not complete, therefore the number of goals and matches are lower than the other years.

The data from this bubble chart is collected from an external API and sometimes may not work properly. The bubbles represent the age arithmetic mean of each La Liga team. Every bubble has more or less the same size, as a result of very similar means between all the teams, comprised from 24 to 27 years old.
